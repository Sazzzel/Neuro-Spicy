Welcome to our Quick Start Guide.

Who is this site for?

This website is for everyone, from every walk of life.

I have made it my mission to build a guide to help people like me.

A guide to help you communicate and understand what Neuro-Spicy people go through on a daily basis and help reduce the frustration, confusion, and tension the not knowing can bring.

What we aim to do.

This website aims to bridge that gap in understanding, by providing relatable scenarios and clear explanations.

People without ADHD often interpret behaviors through a neurotypical lens, which leads to misinterpretations. For example:

Emotional regulation issues: Someone with ADHD may become overwhelmed or frustrated more quickly than expected in certain situations.

Interruptions in conversations: People with ADHD may impulsively interrupt conversations due to issues with impulse control, which can be perceived as rude or inattentive. The website will help clarify that these interruptions are not intentional and are part of the ADHD experience.
